Our Approach

What Kind of Deals We Do

We acquire residential properties in the Tampa Bay area that require renovation or are being sold under market value due to condition, circumstance, or timeline pressure.

Our acquisitions are supported by current comparable sales analysis, a detailed scope of work, and a defined exit strategy before any offer is made. We do not pursue speculative deals. Each transaction follows a documented process — from acquisition through renovation to disposition. Private lenders receive full visibility into each phase.

Capital Protection

How We Protect Lender Capital

Data-Based Underwriting

Every deal is evaluated against current Tampa Bay comparable sales before an offer is submitted. Lender capital is not deployed without a documented margin of safety.

Licensed Title Management

Funds are managed through a licensed title company. We do not have direct access to lender capital during acquisition.

Third-Party Inspections

Third-party inspections are conducted prior to each draw. Lenders verify renovation progress before funds are released.

Builder's Risk Insurance

Every property carries a Builder's Risk Insurance policy for the duration of renovation.

Title Insurance

Title insurance is obtained on every acquisition, protecting lender interests.

Lender Confirmation Required

The exit is not executed without lender confirmation and agreement.
